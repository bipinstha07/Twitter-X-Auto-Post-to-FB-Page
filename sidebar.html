Add group links to share your Page posts with. After posting to your Page, you can select groups in the dropdown to share the post there.

    1 Create Facebook App

    1. Go to Facebook Developers.

    2. Create a Business or Consumer app.

    3. Add Facebook Login for Business product.

    2 Get Page ID

    Go to your Page's About section -> Page Transparency to find your numeric Page ID.

    3 Get Access Token

    1. Open Graph API Explorer.

    2. Select your App and Page.

    3. Add permissions: pages_manage_posts, pages_show_list.

    4. Generate and copy the token.
